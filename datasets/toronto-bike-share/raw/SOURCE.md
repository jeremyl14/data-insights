## Data provenance caveat

The official City of Toronto open data is released with a 2–3 month
lag (e.g. as of 2026-06-11, the portal has data through March 2026
only). Third-party sources like **BikeRacoon**
(https://github.com/mjarrett/bikeraccoon) provide more timely data by
polling the GBFS feed and **estimating** trips from station-level bike
availability changes. BikeRacoon trip counts are estimates, not official
counts — they are inferred from `num_bikes_available` deltas between
polls, and tend to **undercount trips by ~2%** because simultaneous
departures and arrivals at the same station between poll intervals
produce a net-zero change and are missed entirely.

This repo uses the official City of Toronto open data, not BikeRacoon
estimates. If more recent data is needed, the BikeRacoon API
(https://raccoon.bike) or Python package (`pip install bikeraccoon`)
provides near-real-time estimated trip counts, but they are not
comparable to official trip-level records.
